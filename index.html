"Vision is a complex sensory process that enables us to interpret and understand our surroundings through the eyes. It involves the reception of light by the eyes, conversion of visual information by the retina, and transmission of signals to the brain for interpretation. Regular eye care, including check-ups and healthy habits, plays a crucial role in maintaining optimal vision. In a digital era, addressing challenges like eye strain through practices such as the 20-20-20 rule is essential for preserving and appreciating our visual capabilities."
